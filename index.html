    Private policy: We don't collect private information about you [generally]


When you use ac2droid software, like media player or chat messenger in general we do not collect any personal information about you, including name, email address, sex or date of birth. One and only thing we usually need IS access to your current Google account name, which we use in same manner, as HTTP uses Cookies to save your settings. This lets bypass login and verification process, and from security point equivalent to OAuth2 by Google tokens.


Also in some events we can collect anonymous statistics, for example tracking most popular radio etc. In such cases we can make data public, but as stat numbers. Some other apps (like chats) naturally will disclose something about you, and this information you can make public deliberately and on own risk.


Finally, we all live in The Net, so our services not more trustful, then internet in common. For example, as we build on Google Services, Firebase and AdMob, our apps not more secure, then aforementioned services.
